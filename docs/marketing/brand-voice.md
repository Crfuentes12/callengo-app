# Callengo — Brand Voice & Content Guidelines

## Voice Attributes

### We are:
- **Direct** — Say what we mean. No corporate padding.
- **Confident** — We know our product solves real problems. No hedging.
- **Technical but accessible** — We can explain AI calling to a clinic manager or a CTO.
- **Data-driven** — Numbers over adjectives. Always.
- **Practical** — We're the smart ops person, not the hype machine.

### We are NOT:
- Salesy or pushy
- Buzzword-heavy ("revolutionary", "cutting-edge", "game-changer")
- Overly casual (no "hey guys!" energy)
- Generic ("we're passionate about helping businesses grow")

## Banned Words & Phrases

Never use these in any content:
- Revolutionize / Revolutionary
- Game-changer / Game-changing
- Cutting-edge / Bleeding-edge
- Leverage (as a verb)
- Synergy / Synergize
- Disrupt / Disruption (unless quoting someone else)
- "At the end of the day"
- "In today's fast-paced world"
- "We're excited to announce"
- "Unlock the power of"
- "Take your X to the next level"

## Approved Vocabulary

Use these instead:
- "Automate" not "revolutionize"
- "Replace manual calls" not "transform your workflow"
- "Save X hours/week" not "boost productivity"
- "Reduce no-shows by 15-30%" not "dramatically improve attendance"
- "Your SDRs stop wasting time" not "empower your sales team"

## Content Formatting Rules

### LinkedIn Posts (CEO)
- First line = hook (question, bold claim, or counterintuitive take)
- Short paragraphs (1-2 lines max)
- Use line breaks generously
- End with a question or clear CTA
- Max 3-5 hashtags at the bottom
- Length: 150-300 words ideal
- No emojis unless strategic (max 2-3 per post)

### LinkedIn Posts (Company)
- More product-focused, less opinion
- Can include stats, feature announcements, customer wins
- Still short paragraphs, same formatting rules
- Include a clear next step (link, demo, free trial)

### Blog/SEO Articles
- H1 = target keyword + benefit
- First paragraph answers the query directly
- Use H2/H3 for structure
- Include specific numbers and data points
- 1,200-2,000 words for SEO articles
- Internal linking to product pages
- CTA in intro, middle, and conclusion

### Carousels
- Slide 1: Bold hook statement + visual
- Slides 2-8: One idea per slide, minimal text
- Last slide: CTA + brand
- Design: Clean, use brand colors (Deep Indigo + Electric Blue)
- Max 10 slides, ideal 7-8

## Content Pillars

### Pillar 1: Pain Point Education (40%)
Content that names and validates the pain points our ICP faces.
- "Your clinic lost $47,000 to no-shows last quarter. Here's the math."
- "60% of SDR time is wasted on leads that will never buy."
- "Your CRM has 30% bad data. You just don't know which 30%."

### Pillar 2: Product & Solution (25%)
How Callengo solves specific problems. Feature highlights, use cases, demos.
- "How [Company] cut no-shows by 28% in 30 days"
- "What happens when an AI agent calls your leads (real transcript)"
- Feature deep-dives with before/after scenarios

### Pillar 3: Industry Trends & AI Voice (20%)
Position Callengo at the intersection of AI and business operations.
- Commentary on OpenAI, voice AI, automation trends
- "The future of outbound isn't more SDRs — it's fewer, better ones"
- React to news: Bland AI updates, voice tech breakthroughs

### Pillar 4: Founder Journey & Behind the Scenes (15%)
Personal content from the CEO. Builds trust and relatability.
- Building in public moments
- Lessons learned, mistakes made
- Team milestones, product decisions explained

## Audience Personas

### Persona 1: "Operations Maria"
- **Role:** Operations Manager / CRM Admin
- **Company:** 20-200 employees, healthcare or real estate
- **Pain:** Dirty CRM data, manual verification processes
- **Cares about:** Efficiency, accuracy, time savings, ROI
- **Content that works:** Before/after data quality stories, time-saved calculations

### Persona 2: "Sales Director David"
- **Role:** VP Sales / Sales Director / SDR Manager
- **Company:** 10-100 employees, SaaS or financial services
- **Pain:** SDRs wasting time on unqualified leads
- **Cares about:** Pipeline quality, conversion rates, cost per qualified lead
- **Content that works:** SDR productivity stats, lead scoring examples, ROI math

### Persona 3: "Clinic Admin Clara"
- **Role:** Practice Manager / Clinic Administrator
- **Company:** 5-50 employees, healthcare/dental/veterinary
- **Pain:** No-shows destroying revenue and scheduling
- **Cares about:** Show rates, patient experience, staff time
- **Content that works:** No-show cost calculators, confirmation call comparisons
