# ICP & Positioning

## Ideal Customer Profile

### Primary Segments

1. **B2B Sales Teams** (mid-market)
   - 10-200 employees
   - High-volume outbound calling needs
   - Use CRMs like [[HubSpot]], [[Salesforce]], [[Pipedrive]]
   - Pain: Manual lead qualification is slow, inconsistent, and expensive

2. **Appointment-Based Businesses**
   - Legal firms (→ [[Clio]] integration)
   - Healthcare / dental / real estate
   - Pain: No-shows cost money; manual confirmation calls are tedious

3. **Operations / Data Teams**
   - Need to verify and clean contact databases at scale
   - Pain: Outdated CRM data leads to wasted sales effort

### Buyer Personas

| Persona | Role | Cares About |
|---------|------|-------------|
| VP Sales | Decision maker | Pipeline velocity, lead quality, ROI |
| Sales Ops Manager | Implementer | CRM integration, automation, reporting |
| Office Manager | Day-to-day user | Appointment confirmations, calendar sync |

## Competitive Positioning

- **vs Manual calling:** 10x faster, consistent quality, 24/7 availability
- **vs Generic dialers:** AI-powered conversations (not just auto-dial), post-call analysis, CRM sync
- **vs Other AI voice platforms:** Purpose-built for 3 specific use cases (not general-purpose voice AI)

## Integration Strategy by Plan

- **Free/Starter/Growth:** Basic integrations — [[Google Calendar]], Google Meet, Zoom, Slack, [[SimplyBook]], Webhooks
- **Business+:** Mid-market CRMs — [[Microsoft Outlook]], Teams, [[HubSpot]], [[Pipedrive]], [[Zoho]], [[Clio]]
- **Teams+:** Enterprise CRMs — [[Salesforce]], [[Dynamics 365]]
